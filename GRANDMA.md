# GRANDMA.md
## What is IngredientIQ? (Explained simply)

Hi Grandma! 👋

You know how sometimes you open the fridge and think *"I have eggs, some leftover rice, and a bit of soy sauce — what on earth can I make with this?"*

That's exactly what this little website helps with.

---

### What it does

You type in whatever food you have at home — even just a few things like "potatoes, onion, cheese" — and the computer thinks really hard and says:

*"Oh! You can make a Spanish omelette. Here's exactly how to do it, step by step."*

It gives you **3 real recipes** you can actually cook tonight, with full instructions. No need to go to the shop. No wasted food. Just dinner sorted.

---

### How does it "think"?

The clever bit is that it uses something called **Claude AI** (made by a company called Anthropic). Think of it like a very well-read chef who has read thousands of cookbooks and knows how to combine ingredients sensibly.

You ask it a question, it thinks, and it writes back an answer — like texting a really knowledgeable friend.

---

### Is it safe to use?

Yes! It doesn't save your name, email, or anything personal. You type your ingredients, it responds, and that's it. Nothing is stored anywhere.

---

### What's special about it?

Most recipe websites make you search for a specific dish. This one works **backwards** — you start with what you *have*, and it finds the dish for you. It's like having a chef look inside your fridge and sort it out.

---

### Who made it and why?

This was made as a little project to help people waste less food and cook more at home — because most of us already have enough in the kitchen, we just don't always know what to do with it!
